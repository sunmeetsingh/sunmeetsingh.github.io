## üì¶ Cross-Border Logistics Strategy
Shopping from Germany (DE) and France (FR) can save 20‚Äì40%, but logistics are key.

| Shop Origin | Delivery Policy | Strategy for YOU |
|---|---|---|
| **Tikamoon (FR/DE)** | Delivers to CH (Check `.ch` site first). | Use **Tikamoon.ch** for easiest handling. If searching `.de` site for more variety, use **MeinEinkauf.ch** (service that handles DE->CH customs/delivery). |
| **Mazawood (FR)** | Custom atelier in Alsace. | **Contact directly.** They likely ship to CH often. Expect to pay Swiss VAT (8.1%) upon arrival, but *deduct* French VAT (20%) at purchase. |
| **SKLUM (ES)** | Delivers to CH (`sklum.com/ch`). | Direct shipping. Prices usually include customs/taxes, but check final cart details. |
| **Amazon DE / Galaxus** | Delivers to CH. | **Galaxus** is your best friend for appliances (wine fridge) and lighting ‚Äî zero customs hassle, fast returns. |

> **üí° Pro Tip:** For DE/FR shops that don't ship to CH, sign up for **[MeinEinkauf.ch](https://meineinkauf.ch)**. They provide a German delivery address and handle the import/forwarding to Zurich for a fee.

## üç∏ Bar Station (The "Project")

### 7. Console + Wine Fridge
*Target: CHF 1,300 ‚Äì 2,550*

*   **The Challenge:** Finding a ready-made console with a *perfect* fridge cut-out is nearly impossible.
*   **Solution A (Custom/Premium):** Contact **Likoo.ch** or a local "Schreiner" (Joiner) in Zurich.
    *   *Review:* "Build me an oak console (150cm) that fits *this* specific fridge."
    *   *Cost:* High (~CHF 2,000+ just for the furniture).
*   **Solution B (The "Designer Hack"):**
    1.  Buy a **Standard Oak Console** (e.g., Tikamoon) with open legs/shelf.
    2.  Place a **Freestanding Slim Wine Fridge** (30cm width) *next to it* or *underneath* (if height allows).
    3.  *Wine Fridge:* **Galaxus** has brands like **Klimadiff** or **Caso** (30cm wide).
        *   *Search:* "Wine Cooler 30cm" on Galaxus.
        *   *Price:* ~CHF 400 - 700.
