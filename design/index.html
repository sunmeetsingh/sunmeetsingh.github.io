Material Rules
  • USE Oiled oak with flat-faced, precise joinery — no shaker-style panels or farmhouse profiles
  • USE Brushed brass hardware — warm and precise, not ornate
  • USE Structured wool or tight-weave performance fabric on sofas — deep seat, defined silhouette
  • USE Low-pile or flatweave wool rugs — not jute
  • USE Architectural lighting — sculptural metal/glass forms (Gubi, Flos, Louis Poulsen)
  • AVOID Aged bronze — reads antique and will date in 10 years
  • AVOID Jute rugs, Edison cage sconces, linen-shade floor lamps — fights the contemporary floor
  • AVOID Pale sage as a dominant accent — too trend-tied (2023–24)
  • AVOID Loose-covered linen dining chairs — too soft and shapeless against dark modern tiles
