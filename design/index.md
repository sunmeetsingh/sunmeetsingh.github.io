| Area | Low | High |
|---|---|---|
| Living Room | 4,950 | 9,450 |
| Wintergarten | 2,300 | 4,900 |
| Bar Station | 1,300 | 2,550 |
| Master Bedroom | 2,300 | 4,300 |
| Home Office | 1,450 | 3,000 |
| Guest Bedroom | 950 | 2,100 |
| Kitchen + Entry + Baths | 950 | 2,100 |
| Art + textiles + buffer | 800 | 1,500 |
| **Total** | **15,000** | **29,900** |

> **~20k is achievable** by sourcing smartly at the low-to-mid range. Prioritize the sofa, dining table, and bed — everything else can be budget or vintage.
